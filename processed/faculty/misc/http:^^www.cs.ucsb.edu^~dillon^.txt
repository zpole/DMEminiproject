Research interests

Formal methods for specification and
validation of concurrent systems; software engineering; programming languages
Laura K. Dillon received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the
University of Massachusetts in 1984.
She joined  the faculty at UCSB in 1985, following a year as an
Assistant Professor
in the Departments of Computer
and Information Science and of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the
University of Massachusetts, Amherst.
Her research interests center on formal methods for specification
and analysis of software
systems, especially those involving
concurrency.
