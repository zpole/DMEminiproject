Hassan Peyravi

Associate Professor




Dr. Peyravi received an M.S. in Computer Science in 1980 and a Ph.D.
in Computer science in 1985 from the School of Electrical Engineering and
Computer Science, University of Oklahoma, Norman, Oklahoma. Prior to
joining the faculty of the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at
Kent State University in 1985, he was a member of the technical staff at
AT&T Bell Laboratories. His research interests include interconnection
networks for large scale parallel and distributed processing, and computer
communications networks.
