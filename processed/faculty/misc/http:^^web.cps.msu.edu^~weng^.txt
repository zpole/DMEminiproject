John J. Weng, Department of Computer Science

John Juyang Weng




John Weng is an assistant professor of
the Computer Science Department
at
Michigan State University.
His research interests include human-machine interaction,
human-machine interface using vision,
speech, and action; multimedia understanding; learning models for humans
and machines; autonomous mobile learning robots;
self-organizing neural networks; artificial intelligence (especially
learning); computer graphics and virtual reality.
He is a member of the
PRIP
laboratory.
