Biographical Sketch:



Scott B.  Baden received his BS in Electrical Engineering from
Duke University
in 1978 and his
Ph.D. in
Computer Science
from
UC Berkeley
in 1987. After spending two
years as a post-doc at
Lawrence Berkeley Laboratry
, and a year traveling the Globe,
he joined the
Department of
Computer Science and Engineering
at
UCSD
in 1990.  He is also
Senior Fellow at the
San Diego Supercomputer Center.
