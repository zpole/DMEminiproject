Zvi Galil



Zvi Galil
is the Julian Clarence Levi
Professor of
Mathematical Methods and Computer Science at the
Computer Science Department
of
Columbia University
in the city of New York. He currently serves as the Morris A. and
Alma Schapiro Professor and Dean of the
School of Engineering and Applied Science
at Columbia University.
His main research interests are in the design and
analysis of
algorithms
,
computational complexity
and
cryptography
.
Zvi is the Editor in Chief of
SIAM Journal on Computing
and the
Journal of Algorithms
.
