RESEARCH AND TEACHING INTERESTS:
Interests include computer vision, knowledge representation and
artificial intelligence. Recent work has been in the modeling, recognition,
and measurement of objects and has both theoretical and practical aspects.
Applications have been in the industrial, agricultural and medical domains.
In addition to the above topics, teaching interests include algorithms,
data structures, and programming languages.
