Research Interests
John Lu's research interests include pointer analysis and
code transformations that utilize pointer analysis.

Background
John graduated from Yale University in 1992 with a B.S.
in Applied Mathematics.  In the fall of 1992, he entered
the Computer Science Ph.D. program at Rice University and
joined the Massively Scalar Compiler Project.

Current Work

cleaning pass
of the compiler in nuweb.

C-like language.

technical report
on the work
he has completed so far.
