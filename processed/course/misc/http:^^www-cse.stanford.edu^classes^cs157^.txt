CS157

Logic And Automated Reasoning

Description

Introduction to logic for computer scientists. An elementary exposition, from a computational point of view, of propositional logic, predicate logic, axiomatic theories, and theories with equality and induction. Interpretations, models, validity, proof. Automated deduction: polarity, skolemization, unification, resolution, equality. Strategies. Applications. Prerequisite:
109B
.
