Colophon and Statistics for www.cs.wisc.edu



Colophon for www.cs.wisc.edu



www.cs.wisc.edu is currently maintained by
Chuck McKenzie
.
It is a SPARCstation 10/40 workstation with
64 megabytes of RAM and a 1 gigabyte disk.
Our server is NCSA's HTTPd 1.5 running on port 80
with a maximum of 60 concurrent server processes.
User directories are accessed via the AFS filesystem.
A sister server
cgi.cs.wisc.edu
supports users' CGI scripts.
It is a SPARC 10.
Statistics for cgi.cs.wisc.edu are available
here
.

If you make information available through our server, you should
consider using our
statistics reporting
facility
.
